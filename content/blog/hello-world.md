Every developer eventually builds their own corner of the internet. This is mine.

## Why a personal site?

A few reasons:

1. **Ownership** — Social platforms come and go. A personal site is yours forever.
2. **Writing as thinking** — I write to understand things better, not to perform expertise.
3. **A home base** — Somewhere to point people when they ask what I do.

## What to expect

I'll write about things I'm figuring out: software architecture, tools I use, side projects, and occasionally something that has nothing to do with code.

No fixed schedule. No newsletter (for now). Just writing when I have something worth saying.

## The tech stack

This site is built with **Next.js 14**, **Tailwind CSS**, and deployed on **Vercel**. Blog posts are plain Markdown files — no CMS, no database. Simple and easy to maintain.

The source is on GitHub if you want to poke around.
